# DEIXIS LOGO VARIATIONS — Asset Reference

> **Status:** 🟡 CONCEPT STAGE — No final logo has been designed yet.
> This document catalogs the concepts under consideration and defines the format requirements for final deliverables.

---

## 1. Design Concepts Under Consideration

### Concept A: The Frame
- A minimalist square or rectangular frame outline
- The word "Deixis" set inside, small, like an artwork label
- Evokes: gallery wall, exhibition label, framing device

### Concept B: The Pointer
- A subtle arrow or caret — deictic gesture
- Could be an underscore with a dangling serif, or a simple `>` mark
- Evokes: pointing, indicating, "this one"

### Concept C: The Window
- A square divided into four quadrants, one shifted slightly
- Like a viewport or aperture
- Evokes: perspective, framing, looking through

### Concept D: Wordmark Only
- Clean typographic lockup in Geist Sans or a custom letterform
- Lowercase "deixis" with a subtle mark (dot, line, or accent)
- Evokes: editorial, gallery catalog, modern

All assets live in `/public/` or a `/public/brand/` subdirectory.

---

## 5. Design Principles

- **Minimal** — no gradients, no complex shapes
- **Scalable** — recognizable at 16px and 1600px
- **Timeless** — avoid trendy geometry or type treatments
- **Coherent** — mark and wordmark must work together or standalone
