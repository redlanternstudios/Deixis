# Preliminary Deixis — Brand Reference

Source: Preliminary_Deixis.pdf

## Typography

- Titles: Orpheus Pro — refined serif for headings and hero text
- Body: Aktiv Grotesk — clean sans serif for navigation and body copy

## Logo

Gyroscope symbol representing balance and precision. "DEIXIS" wordmark in custom configuration.

## Color Palette

- Black (#000000): Primary text, headers, navigation
- Gray (#6B7280): Secondary, captions, metadata
- Blue (#3B82F6): Accent, links, CTAs
- Brown (#8B6914): Warm accent, edition badges
- Yellow (#EAB308): Highlight, sale markers, featured badges

## Design Principles

1. Curatorial clarity — art is the hero
2. Gallery-grade whitespace
3. Typography-led hierarchy
4. Subtle motion
5. Consignment transparency

## Voice & Tone

Curatorial, artist-first, collector-respectful, Barrio Logan proud.
